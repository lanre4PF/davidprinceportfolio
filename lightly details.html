Lightly

Lightly is an upcoming energy monitoring app and software designed to make energy efficiency enjoyable while helping users achieve their sustainability goals. It offers a variety of features and insights, including real-time energy usage tracking, cost savings analysis, and personalized recommendations. Lightly has requested a brand identity that conveys a playful yet straightforward vibe, making it accessible and engaging for users looking to optimize their energy consumption.

The Problem

Lightly had one major problem.They needed to create a visual identity with a unique and minimalist theme.They needed something that would announce thier company as the latest big boys in town

The Solution

For Lightly, an energy monitoring startup, we wanted to create a brand that feels approachable and relatable. By using simple shapes in the logo and a vibrant mix of orange,Teal , and white, I crafted a visual identity that is both engaging and easy on the eyes.
